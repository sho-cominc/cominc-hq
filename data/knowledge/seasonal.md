# Seasonal Rules — ComInc.

## Pricing Multipliers
| Season | Months | Multiplier | Notes |
|--------|--------|------------|-------|
| Peak | Dec-Mar | 1.3-1.5x | Ski season, highest demand |
| Shoulder | Apr-May | 1.1-1.2x | Cherry blossoms |
| Off-peak | Jun-Sep | 1.0x | Green season base |
| Shoulder | Oct-Nov | 1.1-1.2x | Autumn foliage |

## Content Calendar Themes
| Month | Theme | Content Focus |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Jan | Deep Winter | Powder snow, onsen, winter food |
| Feb | Snow Festival | Events, last chance for best snow |
| Mar | Spring Preview | Season transition, early blooms |
| Apr | Cherry Blossoms | Takada Castle, hanami guides |
| May | Green Season Start | Hiking season opens, rice planting |
| Jun | Rainy Season | Indoor activities, sake, cooking |
| Jul | Summer Peak | Mountain hiking, river activities |
| Aug | Festivals | Local events, family activities |
| Sep | Early Autumn | Harvest, food tours |
| Oct | Foliage Peak | Myoko autumn colors, photography |
| Nov | Late Autumn | Last foliage, sake season |
| Dec | Ski Season Open | Resort openings, winter prep guides |

## Booking Patterns
- Ski season bookings start in October
- Cherry blossom bookings start in February
- Summer hiking bookings start in April
- Plan content 2 months ahead of booking season
